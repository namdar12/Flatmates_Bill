Title: Flatmates Bill
Description: An app that gets as input the amount of a bill for a particular period
and the days that each of the flatmates stayed in the house for that period and returns how much each flatmate
has to pay. It also generates a pdf report stating the names of the flatmates,the period, and how much each of them
had to pay.
Objects: Bill(Object/Class)
            Amount(Attribute of bill object)
            Period(Attribute of bill object)
        Flatmate(object/Class)
            name(Attribute of flatmate object)
            days_in_house(Attribute of flatmate object)
            pays(bill)(Method of flatmate object)
        PdfReport:
            filename(Attribute of PdfReport object)
            generate(flatmate1,flatmate2,bill) expects 3 objects ,2 flatmate objects and 1 bill object.
